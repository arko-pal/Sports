The equipment needed to play badminton includes: 
Net: A net that crosses the center of the court, 
5 ft 1 in high at the ends and 5 ft high in the middle 
Posts: Two posts, 20 ft apart, at the farthest sidelines 
Rackets: Lightweight rackets made of materials like 
carbon fiber, aluminum, graphite, or titanium 
Shuttlecock: Also called a shuttle or birdie, 
this has a cork-rounded base and 16 feathers glued in 
Badminton shoes: Lightweight shoes that offer support and agility
